About Us

Uhlanga books was established in 2019. We specialise in self-publishing.

So far we have managed to publish 9 amazing books filled with great quality and content.

We publish all genres from Poetry,Fiction,Non-Fiction,Short-stories,adult erotic fiction,Romance and Study guides.

The Founder

Motivation Behind Uhlanga Books

When i moved to Cape Town from the Free-State, I resided in khayelistha, I experienced children loitering in the streets with nothing to do and streets flooded with alcohol bottles and cigarettes "stompies",

That for me was a culture shock, i would randomly just close my eyes and envision the whole streets of khayelitsha flooded with books and children reading instead of finishing off alcohol bottles and pretending to smoke.

My main vision is to see books flooding these dusty streets of khayelistha instead. With hopes of establishing children's book clubs around khayelistha, howver the vision does not end there, i would love to have these book clubs around every Township."
